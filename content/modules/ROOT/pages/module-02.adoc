= Module 2: Console + CLI

This module has 2 steps in the OCP Console (UI-required) and 1 step via CLI (Ansible-automatable).

== Step 1: Create a Deployment in the OCP Console (UI — requires browser)

. Go to *{openshift_cluster_console_url}*
. Switch to *Developer* perspective
. Go to *+Add → Container images*
. Image: `registry.access.redhat.com/ubi9/nginx-120`
. Application name: `lab-app`
. Name: `lab-deploy`
. Namespace: `{student_ns}`
. Click *Create*

== Step 2: Scale the Deployment in the OCP Console (UI — requires browser)

. Go to *Workloads → Deployments* → select *lab-deploy*
. Click *Actions → Edit Pod count*
. Set replicas to *2*
. Click *Save*

== Step 3: Verify the Deployment is running (CLI — Ansible automatable)

[source,sh,role="send-to-wetty"]
----
oc get deployment lab-deploy -n {student_ns}
----

Confirm `READY` shows `2/2`.
