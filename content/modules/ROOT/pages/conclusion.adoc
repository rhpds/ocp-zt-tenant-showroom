== How the ZT grader worked

When you clicked *Validate*, the ZT runner:

. Read your credentials from the showroom-userdata ConfigMap
. Used `kubernetes.core` to check for the required resource in your namespace
. Reported pass or fail back to this UI

When you clicked *Solve*, the runner created the resource for you automatically.
